Dear Daddy, ప్రియమైన Daddy,

I don't always know the right words, but I know what I feel. You have been strength, warmth, and quiet love in our family — not just in the big moments, but in the small ones too. A phone call, a smile, showing up when it mattered. I see all of it, and I am grateful for all of it.

నాకు ఎప్పుడూ perfect words raavu, kani naa feeling clear ga undi. Meeru maa family ki strength, warmth, and silent love. Pedda moments lo kuda, chinna chinna moments lo kuda. Nenu anni notice chestha, and I am truly grateful.

On this Father's Day, I want you to know — you are not alone. I am here. You can lean on me, talk to me, and count on me. Our family is one, and I will always show up for you, the way you have always shown up for us.

Ee Father's Day roju, meeku oka thing cheppalani undi — meeru okkare kadhu. Nenu ikkade unna. Meeru naa meeda depend avvochu, naatho matladochu. Mana family okkate, and nenu eppudu mee kosam unta, meeru maa kosam ela unnaaro alage.

You deserve peace, love, and happiness — today and every day. The love you have given still lives in all of us. And I will keep carrying it forward, with you, always beside you.

Meeru peace, love, and happiness deserve chestharu — ee roju kuda, prathi roju kuda. Meeru ichina prema inka mana andarilo undi. And nenu adi munduku teeskeltha, mee side lo, eppudu kuda.

Happy Father's Day, Daddy. Happy Father's Day, Daddy.

With all my love,
Sanjay అన్ని prema tho, Sanjay
